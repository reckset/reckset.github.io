ABOUT US

We love music

Music has always been our passion. After touring and performing across the United States, and recording 2 studio albums we found ourselves settled down in Castle Rock, Colorado working in the corporate world to raise our family. In 2018 we took a leap of faith and decided to re-focus on music. Instead of touring and performing, we found our passion in teaching and sharing our love of music with our students. Just as important as learning about music, we want our students to learn about the joy of performing music for others. We do this by offering multiple performance opportunities each year, and since we have both vocal and guitar students, there are always opportunities to perform with other live musicians.
